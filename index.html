About us

Coruja Janota is a private engineering and economic analysis company delivering bespoke industrial solutions and analysis with auditable environmental documentation and techno-economic integration. We operate with discretion and rigour, serving clients who value confidentiality and long-term industrial value.

Discretely integrating sustainability, economy, and industry, we bring together engineering know-how with financial insight to ensure that bio-based and eco-innovations are not only environmentally and technically sound but also economically viable and strategically future-ready.

Our team has more than a century of cumulative experience in engineering and economics.
